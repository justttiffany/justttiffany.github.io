Scripps Development Team Member

Scripps Institute of Oceanography, UCSD

April 2017 - June 2017

I worked in a team of 4 people under the supervision of a Scripps researcher to continue the development of a Convolutional Neural Network that can categorize and classify recorded fish noises according to species. My task, along with one other team member, was to implement a user-friendly interface using Matlab's GUIDE tool for the CNN to improve usability. In addition, I developed and maintained a team website with one other team member to document progress: underwateracoustics.netlify.com

Task: Implement a user-friendly interface for the Convolutional Neural Network to improve usability.

Technologies/Languages/Development Tools Used: Matlab, Github
