Basically my online resume

Not too long ago, I decided to create a personal website to document who I am and my work.

Overview

In the summer of 2016, I began to learn the fundamentals of HTML/CSS. I had thought to create a personal website about myself, but I didn't start it until summer of 2017. This website will serve its purpose of documenting who I am, showcasing my work, and basically be my online resume (what I can't fit into my resume goes here!).

Skills

I will be actively developing my website using HTML/CSS and Sublime Text as my editor. I will utilize Github to host my website.

Task

My task will be to actively develop and host my website on Github. I will ensure the website is well-organized, easy-to-navigation, and simple yet aesthetically pleasing.

Progress

When I first started out, I coded up my website completely from scratch. However, it was a challenge keeping track of the design and code. The biggest challenge was making the website responsive (it didn't look right on mobile. Oops!). After taking a web design class, I am well on my way to creating a website I am proud of and will continue to update along the way.
