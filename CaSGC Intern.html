CaSGC/UCSD Intern

California Space Grant Consortium

August 2016 - September 2016

I was an intern in the CaSGC/UCSD Summer Internship Academy Program sponsored by NASA during Summer 2016. I worked in a team of 8 people on the Arduino Computer Development and Programming Project to development an Arduino micro-controller for the launching of a near-space balloon.

Task: Develop an Arduino program using temperature and pressure sensors that collected and recorded data for the Near-Space Ballooning Science Package.

Technologies/Languages/Development Tools Used: Arduino, Arduino IDE, Soldering
